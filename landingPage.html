About

Description of the website, how-to-use and other deatails are mentioned below.

Zyper is a secure and flexible website which is used by students across the globe to learn concepts by doing hands-on development/work after learning the particular topic they chose. Mentors will be posting a course based on the topic the kids chose. After completing the course the kids will be given a certain project to complete, based on the course they learnt.

This website is really useful for kids. They will get a hands-on training before working on-site on their desired career paths. The projects/courses which the mentors post are quite detailed and descriptive making it easy for them to learn.
